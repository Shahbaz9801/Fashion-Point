Wholesale FAQ

Common questions about our wholesale process

What is the minimum order quantity?

Our minimum order quantity starts at 50 pieces per style. However, you can mix and match different styles to reach the MOQ.

How long does shipping take for wholesale orders?

Shipping time depends on order size and destination. Typically, wholesale orders are processed within 3-5 business days and shipped via expedited carriers. International shipping is also available.

Can I request samples before placing a bulk order?

Yes, we offer sample orders at a discounted rate for potential wholesale customers. Once you place a wholesale order, the sample cost will be deducted from your first invoice.

Do you offer custom branding or private labeling?

Yes, we offer custom branding options for orders over 500 pieces. This includes custom labels, tags, and packaging. Contact our wholesale team for more details.
